Freelance Strategy · E-commerce · Fashion & Footwear · Pakistan
StepUp — 11.11 Sale Campaign (Clothing & Shoes)
Client: StepUp (Online clothing & footwear brand, Pakistan) · 4-week campaign · $24,000 budget
Case Study ↓

Full e-commerce marketing strategy for Pakistan's 11.11 sale event — the biggest online shopping day of the year. StepUp is a mid-range online clothing and shoes brand targeting fashion-conscious urban shoppers aged 18–35 across Pakistan. The campaign was designed to maximise revenue in a 24-hour flash sale window, supported by a 3-week build-up and a 1-week post-sale retention phase. Channels covered paid social, Google Shopping, influencer seeding, email and SMS, and WhatsApp broadcast. Objective: PKR 15M+ in sales revenue on 11.11 with a minimum 4x ROAS across all paid channels.

01
Build-Up & Wishlist Campaign · Weeks 1–2
Launched teaser ads on Instagram and Facebook revealing discounts coming on 11.11 without showing exact prices. Ran a "Add to Wishlist" campaign driving users to the website to save their favourite items — building a warm retargeting pool before the sale. Activated 10 fashion micro-influencers (15K–60K) for early product seeding and unboxing content. Collected WhatsApp opt-ins and email subscribers with "Get early access" offer.
02
Pre-Sale Urgency · Week 3
Shifted to countdown creative — "3 days to go", "48 hours left", "Tomorrow is the day" ad sequence on Facebook and Instagram. Retargeted all wishlist visitors and website traffic with product-specific dynamic ads showing the item they viewed with a "Going on sale 11.11" overlay. Sent teaser email and WhatsApp blast to the collected opt-in list. Launched Google Shopping and Search campaigns warming up intent audiences ahead of sale day.
03
11.11 Flash Sale Day · 24 Hours
Maximum budget deployed from midnight to midnight. Ran highest-spend Facebook and Instagram conversion campaigns optimized for purchases. Google Shopping at peak bids for all clothing and shoe categories. Sent 3 timed WhatsApp and email blasts: midnight launch, midday reminder, and 3-hour warning before sale ends. Influencers posted live content showing them shopping the sale. Real-time performance monitoring with hourly budget reallocation to best-performing ad sets.
04
Post-Sale Retention · Week 4
Deployed a post-purchase email sequence: order confirmation, shipping update, style tips using purchased items, and a "Thank you" discount for next purchase. Retargeted non-purchasers who visited but did not buy with "You missed it but here's 10% off anyway" campaign. Built lookalike audiences from 11.11 buyers for future campaigns. Collected reviews and UGC from buyers for use in next campaign.
